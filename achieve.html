--Achievements--

These are the systems that I have developed:

  1. ABC-VEN Therapeutic Class Database (AVENT)
    • A programme that analyses procurement records, and end-users' essential ratings to pin-point items most suitable for cost-reduction.
    • Deployed in Hospital Seberang Jaya, and Hospital Angkatan Tentera Tuanku Mizan.
    • Saved RM10,000 in a year.
    • Won 1st place in Penang Health Department Inovation Competition and 1st place in the National Pharmacy Inovation and Creativity Conference.
  2. Never Expire Database
    • A database that keeps track of a store's inventory level and movement.
    • Deployed in Hospital Seberang Jaya.
    • Made the task of monitoring inventory much faster and accurate.
    • Won 1st place in Penang Health Department Inovation Competition.
  3. Antimicrobial Stewardship Database
    • A database that monitors and analyses antimicrobial usage levels. Also analyses the usage patterns of each antimicrobials.
    • Deployed in most government hospitals in Penang.
    • Reduced an antimicrobial's usage by 70%.
  4. CPD Scaning System
    • A database that allows attendees of a CME event to scan their barcode and record their attendance.
    • Deployed in Hospital Seberang Jaya.
    • Made CPD points easier to record, monitor and access.
  5. Project Guardian
    • A system that uses Machine Learning to predict number of MRSA cases based on antibiotics used.
    • Won 1st place in the Health Hackathon Malaysia 2018
